The extent of the bachelor's culinary skills.

Ingredients

While it may be hard to go to a grocery store and find all these complex ingredients, please try your best.

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Find a spoon
  2. Open the newly acquired jars of PB & J
  3. Grab a slice of bread and keep it handy
  4. Proceed to scoop out PB and shove it into your mouth. DON'T SWALLOW YET!
  5. Do the same with the jelly
  6. Now pick up the slice of bread and stuff your face with it.
  7. Voila!
